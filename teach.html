Beginner's Guide to Formula 1

DRS
Drag Reduction System (DRS) helps cars go faster on straights by opening a rear wing flap.
Undercut
Undercut is a tactical pit stop strategy to gain track position.
Safety Car
Used to neutralize the race and ensure safety after accidents or bad track conditions.
Constructors' Championship
The title teams compete for based on combined driver points.
Inside Line
The racing line on the inside of a turn, often used to overtake.
Safety Car Pit Stop
Strategic pit stop during safety car deployment for time advantage.
Telemetry
Data collected from the car used by teams to monitor performance and plan strategy.
